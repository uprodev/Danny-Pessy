From former comedian in Hollywood, to staple in the direct-selling industry, Danny has more than 16 years of door-to-door sales and leadership experience.

With nearly 4,000 total alarm systems sold door to door, there are less than a handful of people who have accomplished that type of individual production. In management and leadership, Danny has recruited and managed more than 100 million in accounts sold.

Danny was one of the first reps in the industry selling alarms, and then adding solar (pioneered in the brief 2016 Vivint Inc + VSLR partnership). He personally sold 80 deals in his first six months selling, and managed more than 300 solar installs in 2020.
