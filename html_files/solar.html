Join Sales Professionals form across the country as we travel to Orosi Valley, Costa Rica to give back to children and families in need. Solar Gives Back has partnered with Globe Aware, a charity company that has operated humanitarian projects.

About an hour from the city of San Jose, in a gorgeous hidden valley (Orosi), rests the tiny community of El Yaz. ALthough the villagers love their natural paradise, they have struggled to make ends meet. Most villagers may not live in abject poverty but have no access to hot water, cars, and many amenities we are accustomed to.

projects

Globe Aware engages volunteers in several different work projects targeted at helping those who are impoverished. Much of the work and projects are related to helping the overcrowded community of Imizano Yetho township where most of the residents live in makeshift shacks (some Westerners would call these – slums) sharing communal taps and toilets with hundreds of neighbors, where the roads are potholed and where the basic infrastructure is run-down. The projects will vary depending on weather conditions, the number of volunteers, and the projects’ needs.

Volunteers will be involved in helping to improve and maintain informal community and pre-school (crèche) care centers, assisting with the refurbishment of the Community Youth Centre, helping to rebuild shacks lost due to fires, replacing simple roofing, home waterproofing, installing concrete flooring, painting and refurbishing local schools, participating in community and schools' sports programs like soccer, volleyball, field hockey, Physical Education lessons. Other projects may include working to improve schools in Imizamo Yethu in Hout Bay, painting murals, and doing other maintenance projects.
