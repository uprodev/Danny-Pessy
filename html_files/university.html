What is Authentic University?

Authentic University is an all-in-one training solution for individuals or teams. We are thrilled to offer our interactive training platform that is designed to keep you current and relevant in the solar space. Our goal is to bring in top-tier speakers who are leaders in the field to share their knowledge and expertise with you.

We understand the importance of offering engaging and effective training modules that allow you to learn at your own pace and on your own schedule. Our training modules are tailored to fit your unique learning style and ensure that you have the tools you need to succeed. In addition, our monthly community calls provide an opportunity for you to connect with Danny Pessy and or his team of coaches to ask questions, receive feedback, and collaborate with other like-minded professionals. Joining Authentic University means taking the first step in shortening the learning curve so you can be more and earn more!
